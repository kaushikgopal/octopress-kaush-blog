> Before you speak, think:
>
> * Is it necessary?
> * Is it true?
> * Is it kind?
> * Will it hurt anyone?
> * **Will it improve on the silence**?
>
> [Sri Sathya Sai Baba](http://www.saibabaofindia.com/sai_baba_quotes_on_silence.html)
